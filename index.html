About

A little about me

I began my career in orthopedic medical devices, progressing from mechanical engineering into product management. I contributed to the design of orthopedic implants, including one that earned a patent, managed product and production activities across roughly 22 products, and worked directly with surgeons and hospitals to translate clinical feedback into product and manufacturing improvements.

Today I work across two affiliated ventures in Los Angeles. At Alimo Architects, I own end-to-end delivery of 3D property-digitization projects, from client acquisition and on-site LiDAR scanning through final delivery. At OpalAi, an agentic AI startup, I lead field validation of AI-generated outputs against real-world conditions and contribute to product strategy, government and commercial proposals, and fundraising, including a successful $2M NASA contract proposal.

My technical foundation spans CAD, manufacturing, control systems, robotics, neural networks, Python, and MATLAB. I'm currently exploring Technical Product Manager and Product Manager roles in AI-enabled products, medical devices, robotics, and other technically complex systems across the United States.
